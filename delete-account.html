Delete Your JotSpace Account & Data

Last updated: May 10, 2026

This page explains how to delete your JotSpace account and the personal data associated with it. You can request deletion either from inside the app or by email — you do not need to have the app installed to request deletion.

Option 1 — Delete from inside the app (fastest)

  1. Open JotSpace on your Android device.
  2. Sign in if you are not already signed in.
  3. Tap Settings → Account → Delete Account.
  4. Confirm the prompt. The app will sign you out and submit the deletion request immediately.

Option 2 — Delete by email (no app required)

If you do not have the app installed, or you can no longer sign in, you can request deletion by email.

Send an email to:

JotSpace@zohomailcloud.ca

Subject: Account Deletion Request
From: the email address associated with your JotSpace account

Please include the following so we can verify your identity and locate your account:

  • The email address tied to your Google sign-in for JotSpace.
  • (Optional) Your JotSpace username, if you remember it.
  • A short note confirming you want your account and data deleted.

We respond within 30 days. If we need to verify your identity, we may ask you to confirm the request from the email address on file.

What gets deleted

When your account is deleted, the following is removed from our backend (Firebase) within 30 days:

  • Your Firebase Authentication record (email, sign-in provider, user ID).
  • Your public profile (username, display name, bio, avatar reference).
  • Your friends list and pending friend requests.
  • Your push-notification (FCM) registration token.
  • Your in-app support-chat messages.
  • Direct messages and group-chat messages you authored, including their end-to-end-encrypted ciphertext blobs on our servers.
  • Your Quiz Hub / Forms / JotSplit / Announcement Board submissions tied to your user ID.

What does not get deleted automatically

Some data is outside JotSpace's servers and you control it directly:

  • Local data on your device. Notes, journals, recipes, expenses, voice notes, and other personal content stored only on your Android device are not on our servers. To delete this, uninstall the app, or use Android Settings → Apps → JotSpace → Storage → Clear all data.
  • Encrypted cloud backups in your own Google Drive or OneDrive. JotSpace stores backups inside your Drive/OneDrive account in an app-managed folder. We cannot read or delete those for you. To delete them, sign in to Google Drive or OneDrive and remove the JotSpace folder, or revoke JotSpace's access at Google Account permissions or Microsoft App access.
  • Group-chat messages on other users' devices. Messages you sent that other people have already received remain on their devices. We replace your authorship pointer with "Deleted user" on our servers, but we cannot reach into other users' phones.

Questions or problems

If you do not receive a confirmation within 30 days, or if you need help with anything above, write to JotSpace@zohomailcloud.ca.
